A Bit About Me...

I'm a wearer of many hats: engineer, designer, writer, and Scrum Master. (If you check out the Hobbies section below, you'll see that I have a whole trunk of non-work hats, too!)

My very first coding class was in HTML and CSS, in high school. I loved being able to make pretty things on a screen, without having to hold a paintbrush or pencil. After that class, I started learning about Java and Object Oriented Programming, evenutally cumulating in a B.S. in Computer Science from American Univeristy. (Also a B.A. in Journalism, but that's another story...)

I currently work as a software engineer at Argon ST, a contractor under Boeing.
